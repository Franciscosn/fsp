# Doctor Level Ideas (1-10)

These are concept-only visual milestones for the avatar progression.

1. `Level 1 - Starter`: Base scrubs and simple shoes.
2. `Level 2 - Kittel`: White coat unlocked.
3. `Level 3 - Namensschild`: Name badge appears on the coat.
4. `Level 4 - Stethoskop`: Stethoscope around the neck.
5. `Level 5 - Handschuhe`: Gloves unlocked.
6. `Level 6 - Klemmbrett`: Clipboard in hand.
7. `Level 7 - Praxis-Set`: Wrist watch and pen tool.
8. `Level 8 - OP-Haube`: Surgical cap unlocked.
9. `Level 9 - Fachaerztin`: Subtle prestige aura + emblem.
10. `Level 10 - Diamond Doctor`: Laurels and diamond particles.

## Suggested unlock logic

- Reach Level 10 only when all cards are in the 7-correct streak state (`Diamonds`).
- Intermediate levels can follow either:
  - total review milestones, or
  - percentage of cards that reached `streak >= N`.
